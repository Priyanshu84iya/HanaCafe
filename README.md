## 🎯 About

Welcome to **HanaCafe (花カフェ)** - a charming digital cafe experience that combines the warmth of Japanese hospitality with delightful pixel art aesthetics! Our virtual cafe offers a cozy atmosphere where you can explore our menu of delicious cakes and beverages, all while enjoying soothing background music and beautiful day/night themes.

## ✨ Features

### 🎨 **Visual Experience**
- 🌸 **Pixel Art Design** - Beautiful hand-crafted pixel art assets
- 🌅 **Day/Night Themes** - Toggle between gorgeous day and night cafe scenes
- 🎵 **Background Music** - Cozy cafe ambiance with music controls
- 📱 **Responsive Design** - Perfect experience on all devices
- 🌈 **Smooth Animations** - Delightful hover effects and transitions

### 🍰 **Menu & Ordering**
- 🥕 **Artisan Cakes** - Carrot, Strawberry, and Choco-banana varieties
- ☕ **Premium Beverages** - Pink Drink, Matcha Iced Coffee, Lavender Milk Tea
- 💰 **Indian Rupee Pricing** - All prices displayed in ₹ (INR)
- 🧮 **Real-time Calculator** - Dynamic order total calculation
- ✅ **Form Validation** - Smart validation for smooth ordering

### 🛠️ **Technical Features**
- 🎮 **Interactive Elements** - Music toggle, theme switcher, smooth scrolling
- 📝 **Accessible Forms** - Proper labels, validation, and user feedback
- 🔧 **Clean Code** - Well-structured HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
- 🌐 **Cross-browser Compatible** - Works perfectly across all modern browsers

## 🚀 Getting Started

### 🎯 **Quick Start**
1. 📥 Clone or download this repository
2. 🌐 Open `index.html` in your favorite browser
3. 🎵 Click the music button to enjoy cafe ambiance
4. 🌙 Toggle day/night mode for different moods
5. 🛒 Explore our menu and place your order!
